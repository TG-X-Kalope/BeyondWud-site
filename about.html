Our vision and business idea

The objective of ECOBOARD is to "provide a better everyday life for many people." "To offer a wide selection of well-designed, effective uPVC Sheets & Boards products at prices so low that as many people as possible can afford them," is our company concept.

We strive to provide our clients with high-quality uPVC Sheets & Boards at reasonable rates by optimising our whole value chain, establishing long-term supplier partnerships, investing in highly automated production, and producing enormous volumes. Our vision is broader than just home decor. We want to make every day a little bit better for everyone who is affected by our company.
